Buffalo Chicken Bites (Instant Pot)

Description

A quick and simple recipe in the instant pot to help jumpstart your taste buds.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 - 2 lbs boneless chicken cut into 1" pieces
  • 2 eggs

Breading

  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper

Sauce

  • 1/2 cup hot sauce
  • 2 tbsp melted butter

Garnish

  • 1 green onion thinly sliced

Steps

  1. In one bowl whisk eggs and in a second bowl mix all breading ingredients
  2. Dip pieces of chicken into the eggs, coat with breadcrumbs. Repeat until all chicken is coated
  3. Select AIR FRY. Set temperature to 375Â°F and set time for 15 minutes. Press START
  4. Arrange chicken pieces into single layer on the cooking tray. Once display shows ADD FOOD, place try into air fryer. Cook bites in batches if needed.
  5. When display shows Turn Food, carefully flip the bites with tongs
  6. While chicken is cooking, combine Sauce ingredients
  7. When cooking program ends, carefully remove chicken, and place pieces into bowl
  8. Pour hot sauce over chicken and toss until evenly coated
  9. Plate, garnish, server and enjoy
