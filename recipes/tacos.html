Simple Ground Turkey Taco's

Description

This simple recipe will help take your taste buds on a trip of flavor. With only a few ingredients needed, this meal can be cooked and served quickly.

Ingredients

  • 1lb. Ground Turkey
  • 2/3rd cup water
  • 1/4 cup of taco seasoning
  • Flour tortilla shells
  • Shredded sharp cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. On a stove top, cook 1 lb. of ground turkey in a pan until thoroughly cooked. Approximately 12 minutes
  2. Drain out any excess grease into a grease pan
  3. Add water and taco seasoning and mix evenly in pan
  4. Reduce heat on stove top and simmer for a few minutes until slightly thick
  5. While simmering, heat up flour tortillas in microwave with damp paper towel wrapped around for 30 seconds
  6. Turn off heat on stove top and scoop taco meat into tortilla shells and garnish with sharp cheddar cheese
