Pumpkin Bread

Description

In the middle of autumn or near the holidays, this delicious dessert will sure be a favorite among the family!

Ingredients

  • 3.5 cups flower
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp nutmeg
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 3 cups sugar
  • 1 cup oil
  • 2/3rd cup water
  • 4 eggs beaten
  • 1 15. oz can pumpkin

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325Â°
  2. Mix dry ingredients together
  3. In another bowl mix oil, water, pumpkin & eggs
  4. Gradually add dry ingredients to liquid mixture. Blend well
  5. Pour into 3 greased loaf pans
  6. Place in over for about 1 hour
  7. Check with a toothpick. If none sticks, serve
